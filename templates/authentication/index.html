ABOUT PROFECTUS
Profectus is a fellowship program by Abhyuday IIT Bombay that empowers young individuals to make a positive impact through social work. Join now for exciting summer internship opportunities in the social sector and become a catalyst for positive change.
WHY PROFECTUS?

Abhyuday’s fellowship program, Profectus offers youth the chance to engage in impactful social work with NGOs, social startups and IIT Bombay alumni. Fellows will gain valuable insights and contribute to society's betterment through diverse responsibilities ranging from communication and impact analysis to fundraising and planning support groups. Under the expert guidance of experienced social workers and leaders, fellows get hands-on experience to develop skills, knowledge, and networks to become an effective agent of change in our community.
